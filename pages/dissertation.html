Predicting the final seeds of National Basketball Association teams, a Machine Learning approach




Predicitive Modelling

Description: This research was conducted in partial fulfilment for the degree of Master of Computer Science and sought to analyse the widely available National Basketball Association (NBA) statistics and attempt to predict the final seeds or ranking of each team at the end of the regular season. The algorithms used to deduce the final predictions are listed below.

1. ELO Algorithm
2. Random Forest Classifier
3. Logistic Regression
